How it works:

• Account registration: Start by registering on our website. Fill in the basic information to create an account or log in to an existing one. By registering once, you get access to warehouses in Dubai, USA, and Canada.

• Getting a personal address in the UAE: After registration, you will receive a unique delivery address in the UAE, which can be used for shopping.

• Shop at any store in the UAE

• Enter your UAE address at checkout: When placing an order, enter your unique UAE address provided by us. The store will send your purchases to this address, and you can add the order to your account on our website.

• Package arrival: Once your order is received at the warehouse, you will be notified at your registered email address.

• Package inspection: Our staff will check the package for the presence of the item and its compliance with the declaration.

• Shipping: Once the shipping fee is paid, the package is sent to the destination address.

• Tracking: Each package is assigned a unique tracking number to track each stage of transit until delivery to the recipient.

• Delivery: The package will be delivered to the specified destination address by EMS courier, to the door. Delivery times depend on the destination country and recipient address.
